SECTION 5 of ACCOUNT STATEMENT

GLOSSARY & EXPLANATORY NOTES

Account ID
The Account ID you set up for your SquirrelSave Account which is usually your personal mobile phone number.

Client Reference ID
Our system-generated unique reference code to identify your SquirrelSave Account. You may use the Client Reference Code to idenfity your account when contacting SquirrelSave.

Portfolio ID
Our system-generated unique reference code to identify the specific SquirrelSave Financial Goal you created.

Portfolio (Financial Goal)
In SquirrelSave, you must set up a Financial Goal before you can start investing through our AI-generated portfolio. Each Portfolio created and managed by SquirrelSave corresponds to a specific Financial Goal you have set up.

Opening Value
Refers to the NAV of your portfolio(s), your cash holdngs with SquirrelSave and your Total Wealth (which comprises all investment assets & cash holdings held with SquirrelSave) on the first day of the period under review.

Closing Value
Refers to the NAV of your portfolio(s), your cash holdngs with SquirrelSave and your Total Wealth (which comprises all investment assets & cash holdings held with SquirrelSave) on the last day of the period under review.

NAV
Refers to the "Net Asset Value" of all investment assets (also interchangeably referred to as "securities"), cash and dividends as of a particular day. Investment assets and cash are valued using closing market prices from the previous trading day.

Deposits
Refers to money that you have transferred into your SquirrelSave Account. The recorded deposit date is the date when all money transfer vetting and checks have been performed successfully and the deposited amount has been allocated into your chosen SquirrelSave portfolio(s) or into your SquirrelCash.

Total Returns
Comprises (i) Portfolio Returns generated by your investment assets in the specified portfolio(s) which includes all dividends, expressed in USD; and (ii) the effect of FX rates (FX Impact) during the period under review which may generate gains or losses when your Portfolio Returns (in USD) are translated into your local reporting currency.

Portfolio Returns
Refers to the returns (after dividends) for each investment asset held in the Portfolio (e.g. security or Exchange Traded Fund) divided by the “time-averaged value of cash invested”. To get that time-averaged value, SquirrelSave assigns each dollar a weight based on how long it has been invested in that investment asset.

SquirrelCash
Non-interest bearing cash holding facility tied to your SquirrelSave Account. Cash that is not specifically deployed by you into any Portfolio (Financial Goal) will be held here.

Dividends
Total dividends paid out by the specific investment asset (e.g. security or Exchange Traded Fund) within the Statement month. Generally, SquirrelSave may reinvest part or all of these dividends within the same month or hold it in the Cash component of the Portfolio.

Cash Activity
Refers to the movement of cash (i) from your desginated bank account(s) into and out of the specified Portfolio (Financial Goal) set up in your SquirrelSave Account; and/or (ii) from your Portfolio (Financial Goal) into and out of your SquirrelCash; and/or (iii) from your designated bank account(s) into and out of your SquirrelCash; (iv) dividends; and (v) FX rate conversions.

FX Impact
Refers to the effect of FX rates (FX Impact) during the period under review which may generate gains or losses when your Portfolio Returns (in USD) are translated into your local reporting currency.

Fees & Charges
Refers to (i) Management Fees (ii) Performance Fees (iii) Custodian Charges (iv) Bank Charges applicable to your SquirrelSave Account.

Management Fee
Refers to the fee payable by you for your Portfolio (Financial Goal) and is calculated per month for the provision of SquiirelSave investment management services for the month preceding the Statement month. Note that ETF management fees are not part of this fee, as they are implicitly charged by the ETF provider and factored into the ETF’s price. Currency FX conversion fees are also not part of this fee, as they are charged as a spread by the converting bank/intermediary, and is reflected in the FX Impact return numbers.

Custodian Charges
Refers to third-party charges paid for and on your behalf by SquirrelSave to safekeep and track your investment asset holdings.

Bank Charges
Refers to third-party charges paid for and on your behalf by SquirrelSave for money transfers between SquirrelSave and your designated bank account. In general, bank charges are payable by you for non-SGD money transfers and for SGD money transfers outside Singapore.

Non-Singapore Tax Resident
A non-Singapore tax resident will be exempt from GST in accordance to section 21(3) of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act.

GST Exempt Fees & Charges
The Fourth Schedule to the GST Act exempts bank charges and FX charges from GST.
